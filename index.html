Localtube

Introduction

This software is simply an interface for using youtube-dl (here) as a video feed downloader. You simply log yourself to youtube and the application will automaticaly download the lattest videos from the channels you follow on youtube. The videos will be saved on your computer as a regular video file.

How to configure

To work the application just need a youtube tocken. For that you simply need to launch the application and click on the big login button. Then loggin to your youtube account and copie and past the code that will be generated into the application (as the Auth code) and let it do it's work.
